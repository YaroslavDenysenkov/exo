# Part 1 – An Introduction to Platform Engineering and Architecture 

## 1 Platform Engineering and the Art of Crafting Platforms
### The demand for platforms as a product
A platform is the combination of different capabilities that are required to master traditional and cloud-native environments so that it supports the end user in the development, delivery, and operation of an application.
